## The challenge

A regional distributor was managing inventory and customer orders across a patchwork of spreadsheets and paper. As order volumes grew, so did the problems: stock counts drifted out of sync, orders were occasionally missed or duplicated, and staff spent hours reconciling numbers instead of serving customers. Off-the-shelf tools didn't match the way the business actually operated.

## What we did

We worked closely with the team to understand their workflow, then designed and built a custom platform tailored to it.

- **Discovery.** We spent time on the ground mapping how orders, deliveries, and stock really moved — and where the friction was.
- **Design.** We prototyped a simple, fast interface the team could adopt without heavy training, focused on their day-to-day tasks.
- **Build.** We delivered a web-based system with real-time inventory, order management, and role-based access, integrated with their existing processes.
- **Rollout and support.** We rolled it out in stages, trained staff, and provided ongoing support as they settled in.

## The results

The distributor now has a single, real-time view of stock and orders. Stock discrepancies dropped by around 80%, orders are processed roughly three times faster, and the team saves more than 20 hours a week previously lost to manual reconciliation. Because the system was built around their workflow, adoption was quick and the errors that used to cost money and goodwill have largely disappeared.

## Looking ahead

With clean, structured data now flowing through one system, the client is planning reporting dashboards to spot demand trends earlier.
